I help women optimize their health and wellness, by finding their UNIQUE nutrition and lifestyle approach, through:

  • Individual and small group coaching and education
  • Data-tracking

My nutrition and health coaching centers primarily on creating an optimal environment and lifestyle to help balance and stabilize blood glucose levels, which gives way to hormone balance, and is highly likely to be connected to:

  • Chronic disease
  • Sleep quality
  • Stress, and its effects on the body
  • Weight fluctuations and accumulated belly fat
  • Unpredictable cravings, moods, increased hungers, energy slumps
  • Insulin Resistance
  • PCOS symptoms
  • Metabolic Syndrome (high blood glucose, high blood pressure, high triglycerides and cholesterol)

I offer nutritional coaching to help guide you through improving or addressing other situations:

  • Gut health concerns (digestion)
  • Thyroid health and hormonal balance through educational support, resources
  • Other specific imbalances or conditions which can benefit from improvements in health and lifestyle habits
