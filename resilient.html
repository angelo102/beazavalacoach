Why RESILIENT Nutrition?

Resilience is largely defined as the capacity or ability to recover quickly from difficulties, spring back into shape; TOUGHNESS.

Basically, it is the capacity we CREATE to be able to cope when things go wrong, are difficult or not the way we envision it, through using unique mechanisms and strength to help us maintain balance and protect our health (physical, mental, emotional, spiritual)

Resilience is active, it requires intention and consistency. Forging resilience forces us to stay away from passiveness and insists we take ACTION, by taking ownership of ourselves and our decisions.

By viewing Nutrition as RESILIENT we are taking our food and lifestyle choices and habits, and creating them uniquely for us, in such a way that they become our body’s armor of protection and provide it with the capacity to bounce back or better cope with the health struggles that come along the way.
