Why RESILIENT Nutrition?

Resilience is largely defined as the capacity or ability to recover quickly from difficulties, spring back into shape; TOUGHNESS.

Basically, it’s the capacity we CREATE to be able to cope when things go wrong, are difficult or not the way we envision it, by using diverse mechanisms, and strength, to help us regain balance and protect our health (physical, mental, emotional, spiritual).

Resilience is active, it requires intention and consistency. Forging resilience forces us to stay away from passiveness and insists we take ACTION, by taking ownership of ourselves and our decisions.

By viewing Nutrition as RESILIENT we are taking our food and lifestyle choices and habits, and creating them uniquely to our own function. Make them your body’s armor of protection. Give your body the ability to bounce back, or better cope with health struggles that come your way.
